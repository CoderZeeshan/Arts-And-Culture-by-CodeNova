Chalukya Dynasty

Pioneers of Dravidian architecture, built magnificent temples at Badami, Aihole, and Pattadakal. Their rock-cut caves and structural temples became UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Hoysala Dynasty

Master temple builders known for intricate stone carvings and stellar architecture. Created architectural marvels at Belur, Halebidu, and Somnathpura with unparalleled craftsmanship.
