About Karnataka Heritage

Karnataka, known as the cultural capital of South India, boasts a rich heritage spanning over 2000 years. From the magnificent temples of Hampi to the royal palaces of Mysore, the state preserves architectural marvels from various dynasties.

These heritage sites showcase the artistic brilliance of the Chalukyas, Hoysalas, Vijayanagara Empire, and Tipu Sultan, representing Karnataka's diverse cultural legacy and architectural evolution through the centuries.
